The Expat Burnout Workbook

There’s a particular exhaustion that comes from living abroad — not the dramatic kind, but a deep, cumulative depletion from adapting all day, every day, to a world that doesn’t run on autopilot for you.

Add the overfunctioning many of us do to prove we can handle it, and you get burnout that hides in plain sight: tired, flat, and ‘shouldn’t be.’ This workbook helps you name it, see where your energy goes, and rebuild a pace you can actually keep.
