Living abroad can be the loneliest kind of full life. The Circle is where you stop carrying it by yourself.

Why a circle

Everyone says “how exciting.” Almost no one asks how you’re really doing.

Friends back home don’t quite get it. The people around you are still strangers. So you smile, you cope, and you carry the homesickness, the second-guessing, and the quiet grief on your own. The Circle is the opposite of that — a small, steady place where the in-between is the norm, not something to explain.
