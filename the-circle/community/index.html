How it works

No scheduled calls. No pressure. Just a place that’s always there.

1

On your time

It’s async, so it works in every time zone. Drop in at midnight or never — lurking is welcome.

2

Start a thread anytime

Carrying something heavy? Post it in What I’m navigating. You don’t have to wait for a meeting.

3

Real peer support

People living the same in-between, who answer with “me too” instead of “how exciting.”

4

A clinician in the room

I’m here as your host — sharing prompts, holding the space, and answering questions. Support and education, never therapy.

The vibe

What keeps it safe and warm.

  • What’s shared here, stays here. Confidentiality is our one firm rule.
  • Come as you are. No pressure to perform being okay.
  • We support, we don’t diagnose. Lived-experience support and education — not therapy or crisis care.
  • Presence before advice. “Me too” goes further than fixing.
  • Be kind; assume good intent. We’re all tired and far from home.
  • Your people are here. Quietly, in the same in-between as you.
