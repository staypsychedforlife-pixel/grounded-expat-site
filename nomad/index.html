The inner side of nomad life.

The gear guides and city lists are everywhere. This is the part almost no one talks about: the rootlessness, the goodbyes on repeat, and the quiet question of what you are really moving toward.

I am Steph, a licensed therapist and digital nomad. I built these guided journals for the emotional side of a life on the move, the freedom and the ache, because both are real, and both deserve your attention. Each one saves your writing privately in your browser, and is yours to keep.
