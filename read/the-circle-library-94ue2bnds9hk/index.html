Listen · Grounding Practices

Twelve guided practices

Short audio sessions, in my own voice, for when you need to come back to yourself. Put in your headphones, press play, and let the pauses do their work.

01

The Arrival Reset

Pairs with · Landing

For the overwhelm of being brand new somewhere. A few minutes to land in your body and catch your breath.

02

Name It to Tame It

Pairs with · The Dip

When the shine wears off and everything feels harder. Naming the feeling gently loosens its grip.

03

Home in My Body

Pairs with · Homesick

For the days you miss home. A grounding practice to feel at home in the one place that travels with you.

04

The Values Touchstone

Pairs with · Who Am I Here?

When you’re not quite sure who you are in this new place yet. A return to what stays true, wherever you go.

05

For the Lonely Nights

Pairs with · Finding Your People

For the quiet, lonely evenings. A little company and warmth until your people arrive.

06

The Self-Compassion Break

Pairs with · Beginner Again

For when being a beginner at everything wears you down. Three breaths of kindness toward yourself.

07

Soften Before You Speak

Pairs with · The Relationship Strain

Before a hard conversation. A moment to settle your nervous system so you can come back to each other softer.

08

One Thing That’s Mine

Pairs with · The Trailing Partner

When so much of this life feels shaped around someone else. A practice to reclaim a corner of it as your own.

09

The Downshift

Pairs with · Running on Empty

For when the tank is empty and everything is too much. Let your body remember how to rest.

10

Savoring Here

Pairs with · From Surviving to Belonging

A practice in letting the good in — noticing what this place is quietly giving you, not just taking.

11

Both / And

Pairs with · Far From Home for the Holidays

For the seasons that hold missing and meaning at once. Room for both, in the same open hands.

12

Who I’ve Become

Pairs with · What’s Next

A look back at how far you’ve come, and what you’ll carry with you wherever you go next.
